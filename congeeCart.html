Menu

Our congee is prepared nightly using super premium Takashi rice (each grain is hand polished and imported from the rice paddies of Japan) and fresh mountain spring water decafiltered drop by drop via reverse neutral drive osmosis. This results in a flavor base that is as exquisitely plain as white on rice. You can then customize your bowl of piping hot congee using these quality ingredients:

  • Zabar Deli & Gourmet Foods Matzah Balls
  • Sakuraniku
  • Orkney Scottish island cheddar
  • Aebleskiver, lovage and parsley
  • Fresh smoked salmon
About Us

Champion Congee began as a side project when Andrew realized he couldn't get hot, wholesome congee on par with what his mom made while working in Seattle as a software developer. After obtaining funding from backers including Gordon Ramsay, Jiro Ono, and Elon Musk, Champion Congee opened at its first location on Amazon's SLU campus serving congee crafted from the finest organic, locally source ingredients. Following critical acclaim, the Chamion Congee truck now caters to a fanatic following around the globe.
